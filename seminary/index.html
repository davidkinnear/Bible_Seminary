Our Purpose
Training the next generation of pastors, church planters and missionaries through doctrinal education and pastoral experience within the context of the local church.
What We Believe
We affirm the Historic Reformed Confessions of faith and particularly subscribe to the Second London Baptist Confession of 1689 as our doctrinal standard.
